Loras Tyrell

Ser Loras Tyrell is a recurring character in the first, second, third, fourth, fifthand sixth seasons. He is played by guest star Finn Jones and debuts in "The Wolf and the Lion." The son of Lord Mace Tyrell and heir to Highgarden, Loras is a popular tourney knight and ranks as one of the most skilled knights inWesteros.
