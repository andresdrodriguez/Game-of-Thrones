Daenerys Targaryen

Daeron was the youngest son of King Aegon V Targaryen. He was betrothed toOlenna Redwyne, but she took a great dislike to his "twitchy little ferret's face and ludicrous silver hair", and their engagement ended when Olenna seduced her sister Viola's intended husband Luthor Tyrell.[1]
